Food




Food is a common denominator around the world. Having grown up in India, I have enjoyed the various recipies of different regions of the country. You are introduced to a plethora of unique spices and styles of cooking that extensively vary in taste and texture. Each region brings with it the simplicity of cooking with basic ingredients to a complex coordination of spices with vegetables and different meats and fish. I have availed every opportunity to try out different recipies, following youtube videos that are readily available.




Subscribing to a number of web site that offer cooking demonstrations with multicultural dishes has expanded my library of different foods. Many a dish has to be scrapped when it did not come out the way it should. I try to be a part of all pot-lucks and taste different styles of cooking. I am proud to say that I have won a few competitions in cooking as well in our office circle.
