Lemon Rice


Ingredients:


2 cups Long grain rice ( Basmati preferably)
(Wash and cook rice, allow to cool and separate)
Juice of two large lemons and ½ lime, add turmeric pwd(1 tsp), salt to taste
Optional ginger julienned, ½ tsp lemon rind.
Mustard seeds 1 tsp
Cumin seeds 1 tsp
1 tsp urad dal (lentils)
2 sprigs of curry leaves
1 tsp channa dal (lentils)
Asafotedia 1 tsp
½ tsp Fenugreek seeds roasted and pounded coarsely
A handful of cilantro chopped fine (Sprinkle on top, toss and garnish)
2 -3 dried red chillies
2-3 green chillies slit in half
1/3 cup peanuts roasted or fried in a little oil
A handful of cashew roasted or fried in a little oil


Method:


In a deep wok add about 1/3 cup of oil and fry ingredients from mustard seeds to curry leaves,
when the lentils start turning a light brown, add lemon juice , let it boil down till the oil starts
floating on top, add rice and stir well. Finally finish with the sprinkle.
